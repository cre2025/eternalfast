# Solar Fasteners: PV Mounting Selection Guide

This guide helps engineers and sourcing teams select reliable fasteners for photovoltaic mounting systems and balance-of-system connections.

## SEO Topics Covered

Solar fasteners, PV mounting bolts, corrosion-resistant bolts for solar, stainless steel solar fasteners, anti-loosening fasteners for PV structures.

## Typical Application Scenarios

- Ground-mounted solar farms
- Rooftop PV support structures
- Tracker system mechanical joints
- Coastal or high-humidity PV projects

## Key Selection Priorities

1. Corrosion resistance under long-term outdoor exposure
2. Stable preload under thermal cycling and vibration
3. Material compatibility to reduce galvanic corrosion risk
4. Standard compliance and documentation for project acceptance

## Recommended Technical Checks

- Material: carbon steel with suitable coating, or stainless steel by project condition
- Coating: HDG, Zn-Ni, or other project-approved anti-corrosion systems
- Joint design: use compatible nuts and washers with anti-loosening strategy
- Validation: salt spray, dynamic loading, and installation verification

## Common Failure Risks

- Using indoor-grade coatings in outdoor solar projects
- Missing checks for dissimilar metal contact
- Inconsistent torque control during field installation

## Procurement Checklist

- Confirm standard and dimensions from approved drawings
- Verify property class and coating specification
- Request traceability package (MTC, inspection records)
- Align packaging and logistics with site environment
