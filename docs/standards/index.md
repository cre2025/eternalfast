# Fastener Standards Index

This index summarizes key standards used in global fastener sourcing, engineering, and quality control.

## Why Standards Matter

- Define dimensional consistency and interchangeability
- Control mechanical performance and safety margins
- Improve communication across buyers, engineers, and factories
- Reduce project risk in international supply chains

## Standard Families

### DIN

German standards widely used in Europe and machinery manufacturing.

### ISO

International standards frequently used for global harmonization and multi-country projects.

### ANSI / ASME

US standards commonly used in North America, often in inch-based systems.

### ASTM

Material and testing focused standards, especially relevant for high-strength and structural applications.

### JIS

Japanese standards common in precision manufacturing and Japanese equipment ecosystems.

## Frequently Referenced Fastener Standards

## Practical Verification Checklist

1. Dimensions and tolerances
2. Thread profile and pitch compatibility
3. Property class or strength grade
4. Material and heat treatment route
5. Surface finish and corrosion requirement
6. Required test reports and traceability package
